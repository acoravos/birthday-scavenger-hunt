The Lost Chapter

Twas the morning of Fred's birthday, when all through the house,
Not a creature was stirring, not even a mouse;
The bed was half empty, his person away,
Because she decided to go to HBS for a 2 year stay.
When out on the lawn there arose such a clatter,
Fred sprang out of his bed to see what was the matter.
Away to the window he flew like a flash,
Tore open the blinds and saw a book half-buried in the grass.
He crept outside, looking around with care,
Not sure if what he saw was really there.
Embossed on the cover was a title he knew:
Anorak's Almanac, shining brightly in the sun's hue.
Fred cracked open the first page and couldn't believe his eyes,
It must be the The Lost Chapter, he surmised.
Anorak's Innovation is unsolved -- not yet put to bed.
He continued on, the first paragraph read:
Hidden keys open secret gates.
Wherein the errant will be tested for worthy traits.
And those with the skill to love even during the straits,
Will reach The End where the prize awaits.
Your journey to Ithaka is not quite done,
Are you ready Player One?
